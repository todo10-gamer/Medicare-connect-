Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

Register as a patient, log in, go to "Book Appointment", select a doctor, choose a date and time slot, describe your problem, and confirm. It's that simple!

Can I cancel my appointment?

Yes! You can cancel any pending or confirmed appointment from your dashboard or the "My Appointments" page before the appointment date.

How do I register as a doctor?

On the Register page, select "Doctor" from the role dropdown and fill in your details. After registration, complete your profile with your specialization, experience, and fees from the Doctor Profile page.

Is my data safe and private?

Absolutely. All passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plain text. All API endpoints are protected with JWT authentication. Your data is never shared without your consent.

Where can I see my prescription?

After your appointment is completed, the doctor will add a prescription to your record. You can view it on the "My Appointments" page — it will appear as a green prescription box on your appointment card.
